Rainbow Florals — Dream Edition (Offline, Flat Pastel)
======================================================

• Fully offline (no fetch) — open index.html directly.
• Soft pastel theme (pink, lavender, blue, no gold).
• New watercolor hero banner (scrolls with page).
• Flower-shaped pastel favicon (fade-in once).
• 6 collections with inline product data.

Customize:

- Edit colors in styles.css (variables at top).
